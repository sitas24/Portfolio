Variables A variable is an element, feature, or factor that is liable to vary or change. For example, if you are using the vending machine, and click a random letter and a random number, then the specific item for the code you have inputed will be chosen. If you choose a different number and letter, you will receive a different item.
Loops A loop is a structure, series, or process the end of which is connected to the beginning. An easy example is walking. When your brain tells you to walk, the left and right leg move in a cycle to move your body until the destination is reached.
Conditionals Conditional statements constructs are used to perform different computations or actions depending on whether a condition evaluates to true or false. For example, if I have a plethora amount of money, then I will buy a lamborghini or else, I will buy a Honda accord.
